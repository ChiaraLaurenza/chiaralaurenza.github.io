I am Chiara — a dancer, yoga practitioner, and terracotta artist. My work lives at the intersection of the physical and the poetic, where body knowledge becomes creative form.

Trained in contemporary, hip hop, and jazz, I bring a somatic sensibility to everything I do. Whether on a dance floor, a yoga mat, or beside a clay workbench, each session is a conversation between movement, breath, and material.

I work with individuals, small groups, and organisations seeking workshops, performances, and commissions that carry genuine depth.
