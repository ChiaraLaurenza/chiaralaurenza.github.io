Practices & Approach

HathaFoundation
A deliberate, grounded practice working with individual postures, breath, and alignment. Ideal for building a strong foundation, developing body awareness, and establishing a sustainable personal practice.
VinyasaFlow
Movement linked fluidly to breath in a continuous, dynamic sequence. Sessions build heat, strength, and a meditative quality of continuous motion, inviting both challenge and release.
SomaticBody-Led
Slow, exploratory movement originating from inner sensation rather than external form. Particularly powerful for dancers, athletes, and those recovering from long-held physical or emotional tension.
